# Example Prompts

## Chinese

- 客户背调：帮我查这个客户，列出证据等级和下一步动作。
- 沟通复盘：提炼这段邮件里的客户事实、风险和跟进计划。
- 起草邮件：基于上面的复盘，帮我写一封可直接发送的英文跟进邮件。
- 销售早报：今天我应该优先跟进哪些客户？
- 报价准备：这些需求是否足够进入报价流程？

## English

- Account research: research this company and produce evidence-backed next steps.
- Meeting prep: prepare a customer call brief with risks, questions, and likely objections.
- Follow-up draft: summarize this conversation and draft a plain-text follow-up email.
- Competitive brief: build an objection-handling battlecard for this competitor claim.
- Quote readiness: check whether these requirements are enough for quotation.
